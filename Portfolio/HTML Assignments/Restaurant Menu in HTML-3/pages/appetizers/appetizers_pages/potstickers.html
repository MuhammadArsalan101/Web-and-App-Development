Potstickers: Pan-fried dumplings filled with your choice of chicken, pork, or vegetable served with soy dipping sauce.

Potstickers are pan-fried dumplings that are commonly found in Chinese cuisine. They are typically filled with a choice of chicken, pork, or vegetables, along with a combination of aromatics and seasonings. The dumplings have a thin and delicate wrapper with a crispy bottom, while the top remains soft and tender. They are served with a soy dipping sauce, which enhances the umami flavors of the filling.
