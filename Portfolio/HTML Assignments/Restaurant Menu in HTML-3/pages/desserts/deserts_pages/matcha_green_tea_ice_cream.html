Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream $3.50
Creamy green tea-flavored ice cream served with red bean paste.

Matcha green tea ice cream is a creamy and flavorful frozen treat that originated in Japan. It is made by blending high-quality matcha powder, which is finely ground green tea leaves, with milk, cream, and sugar. The result is a vibrant green ice cream with a distinctive earthy and slightly bitter taste. Matcha green tea ice cream is often enjoyed on its own or paired with sweet red bean paste for a delightful contrast of flavors.
