Chow Mein $1.50
Stir-fried noodles with a mix of vegetables and your choice of chicken, beef, or shrimp.

Chow mein is a stir-fried noodle dish that originated in Chinese cuisine but has gained popularity worldwide. It consists of noodles, typically wheat-based, stir-fried with a mix of vegetables such as cabbage, carrots, and bean sprouts. Additionally, it can include a choice of chicken, beef, or shrimp as a protein option. The dish is seasoned with soy sauce and other seasonings to bring out the flavors of the ingredients. Chow mein offers a satisfying combination of chewy noodles, crunchy vegetables, and savory flavors.
