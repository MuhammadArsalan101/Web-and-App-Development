Tom Yum Soup $2.50
Spicy and sour soup with shrimp, lemongrass, mushrooms, and lime.

Tom Yum soup is a spicy and sour soup that originated in Thailand. It is usually made with shrimp as the main ingredient, but variations with chicken or mixed seafood are also popular. The soup is flavored with aromatic herbs like lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves. Other ingredients can include mushrooms, tomatoes, and lime juice, which give the soup its tangy and refreshing taste. Tom Yum soup is known for its bold and vibrant flavors, making it a favorite among spicy food enthusiasts.
