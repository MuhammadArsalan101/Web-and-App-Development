General Tso's Chicken $3.50
Crispy chicken tossed in a tangy and slightly spicy sauce, served with steamed rice.

General Tso's Chicken is a popular Chinese-American dish that features crispy, deep-fried chicken pieces tossed in a tangy and slightly spicy sauce. The sauce is typically made from a combination of soy sauce, ginger, garlic, vinegar, sugar, and chili peppers. The dish is often garnished with sesame seeds and green onions and served with steamed rice, creating a delightful balance of flavors and textures.
