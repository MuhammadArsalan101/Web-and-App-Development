Teriyaki Salmon $8.00
Grilled salmon fillet glazed with a savory teriyaki sauce, served with steamed vegetables and rice.

Teriyaki salmon is a flavorful Japanese dish that showcases a grilled salmon fillet glazed with a savory teriyaki sauce. The teriyaki sauce is made from a combination of soy sauce, mirin (a sweet rice wine), sugar, and ginger. The salmon is grilled until it develops a caramelized and slightly charred exterior, while the flesh remains tender and moist. It is typically served with steamed vegetables and rice, providing a well-rounded and satisfying meal.
