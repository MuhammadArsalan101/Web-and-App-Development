Edamame $3.50
Edamame dumplings filled with your choice of chicken, pork, or vegetable served with soy dipping sauce.

Edamame is a simple yet delicious appetizer made from young soybeans that are harvested before they fully mature. The soybeans are steamed and lightly seasoned with salt, creating a savory and slightly nutty flavor. Edamame is often served in the pod, and diners can simply squeeze the beans out with their fingers and enjoy them as a healthy and addictive snack.
