Mango Sticky Rice $4.50
Sweet glutinous rice topped with fresh mango slices and coconut sauce.

Mango sticky rice is a popular Thai dessert that combines the sweetness of ripe mangoes with the creamy texture of glutinous rice. The sticky rice is cooked in coconut milk and sweetened with sugar, giving it a luscious and aromatic flavor. It is served with slices of fresh mango on top and drizzled with a coconut sauce for added richness. The combination of flavors and textures makes this dessert a refreshing and indulgent treat.
