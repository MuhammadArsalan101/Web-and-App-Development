Tempura Ice Cream $6.50
Deep-fried ice cream with a crispy coating, served with chocolate or caramel sauce.

Tempura ice cream is a unique dessert that combines the contrasting elements of hot and cold, crispy and creamy. It starts with a scoop of ice cream, usually vanilla, which is coated in a tempura batter and quickly deep-fried until the outer shell becomes crispy while the ice cream inside remains frozen. The dessert is often served with a drizzle of chocolate or caramel sauce, adding an extra layer of sweetness and decadence.
