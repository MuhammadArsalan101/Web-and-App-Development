Pad Thai $6.50
Stir-fried rice noodles with shrimp, chicken, tofu, bean sprouts, and peanuts in a tamarind-based sauce.

Pad Thai is a well-known Thai stir-fried noodle dish that is both sweet and tangy. It typically consists of rice noodles stir-fried with shrimp, chicken, tofu, bean sprouts, and scrambled eggs. The noodles are flavored with a tamarind-based sauce that combines elements of sweetness, sourness, and umami. Crushed peanuts and a squeeze of lime are often added for additional texture and freshness.
