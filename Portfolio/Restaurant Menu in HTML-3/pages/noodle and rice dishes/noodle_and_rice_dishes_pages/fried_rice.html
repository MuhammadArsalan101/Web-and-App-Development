Fried rice is a versatile dish found in many Asian cuisines, including Chinese, Thai, and Japanese. It is made by stir-frying cooked rice with a combination of ingredients such as vegetables, meat, seafood, and eggs. The dish is typically seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and other seasonings to enhance its flavor. Fried rice is known for its vibrant colors, varied textures, and savory taste, making it a popular and satisfying choice.
