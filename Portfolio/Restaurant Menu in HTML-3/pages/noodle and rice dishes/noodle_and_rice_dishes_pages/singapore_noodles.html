Singapore Noodles $3.00
Vermicelli noodles stir-fried with curry powder, shrimp, chicken, and a variety of vegetables.

Singapore noodles, despite the name, are not actually a traditional dish from Singapore but are commonly found in Chinese takeout menus. They are made by stir-frying vermicelli noodles with a variety of ingredients, including shrimp, chicken, curry powder, bean sprouts, bell peppers, and onions. The dish is seasoned with soy sauce and spices, giving it a slightly spicy and fragrant taste. Singapore noodles offer a delightful mix of textures and flavors, making them a popular choice for noodle lovers.
