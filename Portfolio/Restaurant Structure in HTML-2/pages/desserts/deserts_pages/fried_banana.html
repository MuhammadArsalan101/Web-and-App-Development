Fried Banana $4.50
Crispy banana fritters served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and drizzled with honey.

Fried banana, also known as banana fritters, is a popular dessert in various Asian cuisines. It involves coating ripe bananas in a sweet batter, typically made from flour, sugar, and spices. The battered bananas are then deep-fried until golden and crispy. The dessert is often served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and drizzled with honey or caramel syrup, providing a delightful contrast between the warm, crunchy exterior and the cold, creamy ice cream.
