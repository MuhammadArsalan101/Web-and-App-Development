Beef and Broccoli $6.50
Sliced beef stir-fried with broccoli and a savory sauce, served with steamed rice.

Beef and broccoli is a classic Chinese stir-fry dish that features thinly sliced beef cooked with broccoli florets in a savory sauce. The beef is typically marinated and stir-fried until it becomes tender and flavorful. The dish is completed with a sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and oyster sauce, which coats the beef and broccoli, creating a delicious blend of savory and slightly sweet flavors. It is commonly served with steamed rice.
