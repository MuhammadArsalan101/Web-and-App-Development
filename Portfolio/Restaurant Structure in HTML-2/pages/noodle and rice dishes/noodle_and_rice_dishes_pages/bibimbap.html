Bibimbap: Korean rice dish with assorted sautéed vegetables, beef, and a fried egg, served with spicy sauce.

Bibimbap is a traditional Korean rice dish that translates to "mixed rice." It consists of a bowl of steamed rice topped with various sautéed and seasoned vegetables, thinly sliced beef, and a fried egg. The dish is typically served with a spicy sauce called gochujang, which adds a kick of heat and flavor. Before eating, all the ingredients are mixed together to create a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Bibimbap offers a balance of fresh and cooked vegetables, tender meat, and the richness of a perfectly fried egg.
