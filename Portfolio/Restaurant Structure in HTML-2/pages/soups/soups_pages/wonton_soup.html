Clear broth soup with pork or shrimp dumplings, vegetables, and noodles.

Wonton soup is a classic Chinese soup made with a clear broth, pork or shrimp-filled wontons, vegetables, and noodles. The wontons are small dumplings that are filled with seasoned ground pork or shrimp. The broth is typically made from chicken or pork bones, simmered with aromatics like ginger and green onions. The soup is often garnished with leafy greens, such as bok choy, and it provides a comforting and satisfying start to a meal.
